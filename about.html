About me

Born and raised in vibrant Colombia, I've always been fascinated by the limitless possibilities of computer and web development. Self-taught and always eager to learn, I've spent countless hours honing my skills in this exciting field.

Recently, I made the decision to pursue a degree in software engineering at Politecnico Gran Colombiano. It was a choice I made with great conviction after realizing I wanted to take my skills to the next level and become a professional in this field, and I've been working hard ever since to acquire the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in this dynamic industry.

Although my journey in software engineering has just begun, I'm eager to take the next step in my career and put my skills to work in a professional setting. If you're in search of a dedicated and driven developer with a strong work ethic and a passion for excellence, I would be honored to hear from you.
